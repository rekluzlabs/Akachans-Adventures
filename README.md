#  Akachan’s Adventures

**Akachan’s Adventures** is a charming 2D arcade game where you guide a curious baby girl crawling through her Japanese home on a magical, bubble-filled journey.

## Gameplay

Explore a variety of cozy household rooms as bubbles float gently overhead. Each bubble holds a surprise—use the baby’s trusty rattle to pop them and release falling milk bottles. 
Catch them to earn points and unlock special power-ups that help her progress.

But it’s not all peaceful…

A mischievous household robot vacuum roams the floors, constantly getting in her way. If it gets too close, it will slow her down and make it harder to keep up her momentum.

Can you help her make it all the way through the house?

## Features

* Simple, responsive controls
* Satisfying bubble-popping mechanics
* Combo-based scoring system
* Power-ups from collected milk bottles
* Dynamic obstacle: the roaming robot vacuum
* Multiple rooms to explore within a Japanese home setting
